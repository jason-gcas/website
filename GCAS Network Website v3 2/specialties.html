Why These Ten

Chosen Because Government Needs Them Every Single Day.

Agencies do not select trades at random. They issue recurring, predictable, multi-year contracts for the services that keep facilities operating. These ten specialties represent the bulk of that recurring demand — and they are where a well-prepared small business can build durable revenue.

01 · Recurring

Multi-Year IDIQ Volume

Federal facility maintenance contracts are awarded in 3- to 5-year Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity vehicles — the kind of revenue visibility that lets a small business scale confidently.

02 · Set-Aside

Small Business Preferred

Every specialty here falls squarely inside the 23% federal small-business set-aside mandate — often with additional preferences for 8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, and WOSB designations.

03 · Scalable

Room to Grow Vertically

Each discipline has a defined ladder from first municipal award → state-level IDIQ → federal prime contract. GCAS maps the ladder and accompanies you up each rung.
