Our Engagement Model

Services & Fees

Strategic procurement support built on a performance-based model. We win when you win.

Exclusive Territory Representation

GCAS LLC supports contractors in strategically pursuing and winning government contracts at the federal, state, and local levels. For each defined territory, we align with one qualified contractor, providing structured and exclusive representation on relevant solicitations.

Our role is to strengthen your competitive positioning, ensure compliance alignment, and manage a disciplined, strategic approach throughout the procurement process.

Getting Procurement-Ready

If your company moves forward as a GCAS client, our first step is to ensure you are fully procurement-ready. This includes securing and/or optimizing key registrations such as UEI and CAGE codes, SBA designations, and developing core materials like your Capability Statement and internal documentation.

We will monitor opportunities daily within your assigned NAICS codes across your territory of operation. For each relevant opportunity, you will receive a one-page executive summary for review.

Strategic Bid Support

If you choose to pursue a bid, we will provide the full solicitation package along with pricing intelligence to ensure your submission aligns with market expectations set by the issuing agency.

On every bid you pursue, we will work closely with you, applying the GCAS method to ensure your submission is both compliant and highly competitive.

Fee Structure

Success Fee

1.5% of Gross Contract Value

Paid only upon award. When you win, we win.

Retainer

$2,000

Covers initial setup and strategic positioning, including:

  • UEI & CAGE registration
  • SBA set-aside compliance alignment
  • Development of a professional Capability Statement
  • Full on-boarding into our bid monitoring system

Monthly Service Fee

$500

Begins in month two and covers ongoing monitoring, opportunity sourcing, and exclusive representation.

Fee Credit Structure

To align incentives, both the retainer and monthly service fees are credited toward the success fee upon contract award.

For example: If you are awarded a $1,000,000 contract, the success fee would be $15,000 (1.5%). If $5,000 has already been paid (retainer + monthly fees), your net success payment would be $10,000.

Ongoing monthly fees will continue to be credited toward future success fees.
